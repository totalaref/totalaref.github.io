Total Annihilation Installation and Patching

Welcome to the definitive guide to installing and playing Total Annihilation!
Let's get started; firstly if you are looking to play online, the first thing you will do is install Total Annihilation from Steam, GOG or from the original CDs.

Steam Option:
 Total Annihilation will by default be installed to `C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Total Annihilation` The best advisable option is to make a full folder copy of this "Total Annihilation" directory to another place on your computer; preferably on your C Drive in a non-Adminstrator accessible folder.

GOG Option:
 Total Annihilation will by default be installed to `C:\GOG Games\Total Annihilation` The best advisable option is to make a full folder copy of this "Total Annihilation" directory to another place on your computer; preferably on your C Drive in a non-Adminstrator accessible folder.

Patching Total Annihilation (OTA)


 To patch the original game to play online requires at minimum the Unofficial Patch. The latest version can be found here https://github.com/vThaldren/TA-Unofficial-Patch-Install/releases with the `OTA.Dropin.Install.zip` The patch includes many fixes and quality of life improvements to Total Annihilation. The patch also utilizes CNC-DDraw to improve rendering performance. Extract all contents of `OTA.Dropin.Install.zip` into your designated `Total Annihilation` directory and overwrite all files.

Playing Online

TAF - Total Annihilation Forever
 TA Forever is a multiplayer client for Total Annihilation for playing online. TA Forever can be downloaded from here https://www.taforever.com/ All you need is five minutes to download the client and create an account to play online.
